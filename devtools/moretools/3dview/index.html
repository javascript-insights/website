Piet Mondrian

Learn more on Wikipedia

Piet Mondrian was a Dutch painter who is known for being one of the pioneers of 20th-century abstract art, as he changed his artistic direction from figurative painting to an increasingly abstract style, until he reached a point where his artistic vocabulary was reduced to simple geometric elements.
