3D View Tool Demonstration

Instructions

Welcome to the 3D View Tool demonstration! Follow the steps below to explore the capabilities of the 3D View tool in your browser's DevTools:

  1. Open your browser's DevTools (usually by pressing F12 or Ctrl+Shift+I).
  2. Navigate to the "3D View" tool.
  3. Use the checkboxes and dropdown options to explore different features of the 3D View tool.
  4. Observe how the elements on this page are rendered in a 3D perspective.
