Autofill Tool Demo

Instructions

Welcome to the Autofill Tool Demo! This page demonstrates the capabilities of the Autofill tool in browser DevTools. Follow the steps below to explore each feature:

  1. Open your browser's DevTools (usually by pressing F12 or Ctrl+Shift+I).
  2. Navigate to the "Application" tab.
  3. Expand the "Storage" section and select "Autofill" to inspect saved addresses.
  4. Use the checkboxes and dropdown options to filter and debug saved addresses.
