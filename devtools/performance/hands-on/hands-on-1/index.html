Instructions

In this hands-on exercise, you will learn how to measure and optimize the Largest Contentful Paint (LCP) metric using browser devtools.

  1. Open the browser devtools by pressing F12 or Ctrl+Shift+I (Windows/Linux) or Cmd+Opt+I (Mac).
  2. Navigate to the "Performance" tab.
  3. Click the "Record" button to start recording the performance.
  4. Reload the page to capture the loading performance.
  5. Stop the recording after the page has fully loaded.
  6. Analyze the performance recording to identify the LCP element and its load time.

Tasks

  • Identify the LCP element on the page and note its load time.
  • Optimize the LCP by reducing the load time of the largest content element.
  • Compare the LCP before and after optimization to see the improvement.
