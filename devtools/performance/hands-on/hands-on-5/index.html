Understanding Network Activity in Performance Profiles

The NET section in the Performance panel shows how network activity impacts your application's overall performance.

Quick Instructions

  1. Open DevTools (F12)
  2. Go to the Performance panel
  3. Enable Network and Screenshots in settings ⚙️
  4. Click Record ⏺️
  5. Use the buttons below
  6. Click Stop and analyze the results
