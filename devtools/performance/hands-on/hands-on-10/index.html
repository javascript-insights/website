Performance Tool Hands-On

Performance Hands-On Instructions

  1. Open DevTools and switch to the "Performance" or "Performance Insights" tab.
  2. Click "Record" and then use the buttons to fetch data or trigger events.
  3. Stop recording and review the main thread activity, CPU usage, and FPS.
  4. Identify scripting, rendering, and painting events in the Timeline panel.
  5. Experiment with color changes and animations to examine paint operations.
